Step 1

Prove that the google matrix \(G\) is a stochastic matrix.

Recall the three axioms of probability:
  1. \(0 \leq P(E) \leq 1\) [\(E\) is an event]
  2. \(P_n(S)=1\) [Probability of the Sample space occurring]
  3. \(\displaystyle\sum^{n=1}_{i=1} P(E_i)=1 \)
A stochastic matrix is a square matrix of nonnegative real numbers such that the sum of the entries in each column is equal to 1. This is true for the google matrix \(G\), as shown by the implementation of the command below.

>> GMatrix(.15,1)
